About

We're a group of like-minded gamers in search of competition. This all began with a small number of friends looking to see whos the best, so we got together and organized a small tournament. More and more people began joining our gatherings so we decided to open it up to the public. Ever since then we've organized multiple events in the Dublin city center area. Currently we are only hosting events for Dota 2 and Counter Strike Global Offensive but we have plans to expand in the near future. You can find more details about our events by going to the "Tournaments" section.

Watch the video below if you'd like to check out the vibe of our events.
